Who am I...

I'm Martina and I welcome you to the magical plant world! My story has two beginnings: The first, in which as a child I fall in love with the world of forests and meadows, when I enter the magical world of medicinal plants with my grandmother and grandfather. That was my true happy place. The second, when in the vortex of the covid pandemic, in March 2020, I lose my job overnight. Then I decided to turn the loss of the ground under my feet into an opportunity for my new beginning. I am opening my small business for the production of essential oils and in my home business, with the support of my husband Marko, I am starting the production of the first drops and hydrolates as a by-product. Three years later, Aroma Natura becomes a real family brand, and one of the best in the region that produces organic essential oils of high quality.
